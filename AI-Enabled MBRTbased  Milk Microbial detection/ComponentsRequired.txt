HEATING CIRCUIT :

ESP8266 microcontroller – For temperature control and relay switching.

DS18B20 temperature sensor – For precise temperature monitoring.

5V Relay module – To control the heater circuit.

NPN transistor – For driving the relay.

Nichrome wire – Used as the heating element.

12V Adapter – Power supply for the heater.

5V Buck Converter – To step down 12V to 5V for ESP8266 and relay module.
